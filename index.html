About The Monkey King

Wukong is a vastayan trickster who uses his strength, agility, and intelligence to confuse his opponents and gain the upper hand.

Abilities

The abilities used in game

  • Passive - Stone Skin
  • Q - Crushing Blow
  • W - Warrior Trickster
  • E - Nimbus Strike
  • R(ULT) - Cyclone

Ability Description

Information on Wukong's Moves

  1. Passive - Gains armor the more he attacks you
  2. Q - Gains attack range and bonus damage on his next basic move
  3. W - Dashes and becomes invisible leaving a clone that attacks enemies
  4. E - Dashes to an enemy and sending out images to attack
  5. R(ULT) - Wukong's extends his staff and spins it around repeatedly, gaining Movement Speed. Enemies struck take damage and are knocked up.
